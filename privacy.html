Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

CodeBase is an educational platform designed by students for university students. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google Authentication (Firebase) to verify your university student status. When you log in, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture provided by Google.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to provide access to university course materials and to personalize your dashboard experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Security

We use Firebase, a platform by Google, to ensure your data is stored securely. We do not store passwords; authentication is handled entirely by Google's secure servers.

4. Educational Purpose

CodeBase is a non-commercial, student-led project. Our only goal is to facilitate better access to learning resources for our peers.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us through the official university channels.
